Surale tale

Both of my parents are from Tatarstan, a small republic in Russia, and ever since I was a child I would spend all of my school summer holidays there. My grandmother has taught me tatar language by reading me traditional tatar folklore. I was raised on these stories and would like to share my heritage and culture with the rest of the world. The idea behind this project was to create an interactive story about Şüräle, a forest spirit. This project was created in p5js, using illustrations created by Baynazar Almenov.
